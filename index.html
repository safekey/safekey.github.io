The problem with campus security

Campus crime is on the rise. While the number of reported arrests has been decreasing steadily, the strong feeling among students is that they are not safe on campus. This feeling is due to the outdated and ineffective ways students must report crimes. Cell phones are carried by nearly every college student, but they are also the number one targeted item by thieves and assailants. With the unsettling feeling shared by our fellow students, faculty, and parents of students, we set out to solve the growing problem of campus safety, and restore peace-of-mind by creating Safekey - the world's fastest and only internet-connected crime response device.

Only 29% of students say they feel safe on campus

Surveys taken at metropolitan campuses reveal that students are not comfortable with the level of safety they are receiving.

Students are regularly targeted by non-student criminals

Many campuses are directly targeted by criminals who seek to take advantage of younger population present on those campuses.

Female students are at significant risk for sexual assault

Females are more than 200% more likely to be the victim of a sexual assault on a campus, and have no way of reacting quickly to a bad situation.

Police response times are simply too long

Police cars can only go so fast, luckily, the time it takes for them to receive the call can be drastically improved.

Many spots on campus have little to no visibility

When a victim's mobile phone is taken, they are left without connection to the outside world, and often in a place where nobody will hear or see them.

No battery, no help.

Mobile phones lack sufficient battery life. If walking home alone, a student should not have to rely on their battery to save their life.

Phones are the #1 stolen item

Most often, phones are targeted. They are stolen, or simply destroyed. Keys are not taken often, as stolen vehicles are much less common than purses and phones.

Most crimes happen where nobody can hear them

Most campus crimes are silent. When nobody can hear you, it's difficult to reach out for help.

Our solution

Making emergency response as easy as locking a car

We thought it was crazy that unlocking your car is faster and more reliable than calling for help in an emergency. So we invented Safekey, an internet-connected key fob that gives you real-time access to police. At the push of a button, police not only know who you are, but where you are, and where you will be. The process of unlocking a mobile phone, finding your dialer, calling 911, and describing your situation can now be done from your pocket.

Safekey was designed by students, for students

Safekey runs on a rock-solid distributed system. This means we're there for you when nobody else is. Surveys suggest that notifying friends is among the most common form of protection amongst students. While this is a good idea, friends are usually not as reliable as full-time officers, and can be slow to react. Safety is everyone's concern - with Safekey, it's our business.

Seamless integration with local law enforcement

Mobile apps that notify your family if you are in danger exist, and they work well... for notifying your family and friends. What students really need is a way to discreetly and easily notify authorities that something just isn't right. The majority of crimes could be stopped if someone had simply reported them. The issue still exists, students don't report crimes. With Safekey, it's safer and easier than ever to report crimes! Because Safekey is directly integrated with local police, students can rest assured their calls will be answered, REST API calls that is.

Why top universities support Safekey

It's lightening fast

From button push to police receiving the message - Safekey has a round-trip time of 3 seconds. That means police know who is in trouble, that they're in trouble, and where they are located within 3 seconds of button push.

It's fully secure

Powered by a secure cloud platform, Safekey stores no plain-text data. Our platform is secure from end-to-end, keeping you safe both physically and digitally.

Directly connected to emergency networks

Safekey notifies local authorities who are nearest to the emergency. Safekey doesn't notify family members who live hundreds of miles away.

24/7 Law Enforcement Monitoring

Safekey is monitored at all hours of the day, making sure someone is always there when needed.

Location relayed to police in real time

When pushed, Safekey relays location to law enforcement in real-time - preventing kidnappings, purse snatchings, and other kinds of crimes.

Rare quality at a very common price

Safekey devices are produced with quality in mind. Luckily, this quality doesn't have to come at a high price. Safekey devices are manufactured cheaply thanks to modern semiconductor technology.
